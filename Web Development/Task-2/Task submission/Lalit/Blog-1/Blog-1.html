About my experience in school

Hello everyone!
I am Lalit Kumar. I joined DPS Kalinga in 2006 in class Prep and went with so many memories till class 12. As we all now school days are the best and everyone of us find the best time of our life there. Meeting our friends daily , running to the field in the breaktime and sharing tiffin were some of my favourite moments.


School Info

School: Delhi Public School

It has a magnificient infrastructure with many caring and highly qualified teachers. DPS Kalinga conducts sports meet, science exhibition and many other activities throughout the year to engage students.

The learning environment here is very good for all groups of students , specially for children.


The Ampitheatre

The Ampitheatre is very special for all the students of DPS Kalinga. Every thursday morning in the school starts with a morning assembly

Principal Ma'am comes , does the prayer and the begins the assembly. Students perform plays , sing song and dance and there is news session.These activities are then followed by prize distribution. For students standing in the sun was fun. House representatives and teachers were actively involved in seaching for long nails and in checking school uniform. That was so much fun!!
